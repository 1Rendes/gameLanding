FAQ

  • How do I start playing Water Splash?

    Download from the Play Store and press 'Play'.

  • Is this game free?

    Yes, but it offers optional in-app purchases.

  • Can I play with friends?

    Yes, you can connect with friends, compare progress, and compete on the leaderboard.

  • How often are new levels added?

    New levels and challenges are added regularly with updates.

  • What are boosters, and how do I get them?

    Boosters can be earned by completing challenges or purchased in-game to help clear tough levels.

  • Can I play without an internet connection?

    Yes, Water Splash supports offline play.
