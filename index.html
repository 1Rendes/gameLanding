About This Game

Water Splash is a fun and challenging match-3 puzzle game where players match water-themed items like balloons, fish, and more to clear levels and make a splash! With hundreds of levels, stunning graphics, and regular updates, this game will keep you entertained for hours. Explore beautiful water landscapes, use powerful boosters, and beat the toughest puzzles as you advance through each level.

How to play

  • Match Water-Themed Items: Match three or more water balloons or other items to clear them from the board.

  • Earn Stars: Complete levels with fewer moves to earn more stars.

  • Complete Objectives: Each level has a unique goal—complete it to advance.

  • Use Boosters: Unlock special boosters to help you clear difficult levels.

Unlock New Challenges: As you progress, face new obstacles and challenges, keeping the gameplay fresh
